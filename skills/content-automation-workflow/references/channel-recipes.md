# Channel Recipes

Use this file when the workflow must produce channel-specific outputs.

## WeChat Article

### Best for
- long-form explanation
- structured观点输出
- research synthesis
- high-trust content

### Format guidance
- strong title
- lead paragraph with immediate value
- 3 to 6 structured sections
- explicit conclusion or action takeaway
- cover image and inline illustrations when useful

### Production pairing
- `公众号推文发布助手`
- `baoyu-post-to-wechat`
- `baoyu-image-gen`
- `baoyu-cover-image`
- `baoyu-article-illustrator`

### Notes
- prefer draft-first
- treat the WeChat article as a canonical public long-form output

---

## Feishu Doc / Wiki

### Best for
- internal knowledge deposition
- article notes
- reusable structured knowledge
- collaboration and archival

### Format guidance
- one doc per topic when the content is substantial
- clear sections and reusable bullets
- include source links, summary, key points, and follow-up ideas

### Production pairing
- `feishu_doc`
- `feishu_wiki`
- `memory-deposit`

### Notes
- use this as a durable internal asset layer
- do not treat it as an afterthought

---

## Xiaohongshu

### Best for
- punchy insights
- card-style content
- creator-friendly visual repurposing
- emotionally resonant framing

### Format guidance
- stronger hook
- fewer ideas per piece
- more visual rhythm
- shorter sections
- card or image support often matters more than depth

### Production pairing
- `baoyu-xhs-images`
- `baoyu-image-gen`
- `baoyu-format-markdown`

### Notes
- do not dump a WeChat article into Xiaohongshu unchanged
- rewrite for speed, clarity, and visual flow

---

## Weibo

### Best for
- fast commentary
- opinion spikes
- hot-topic distribution
- short public reactions

### Format guidance
- lead with the strongest idea
- compress hard
- one post = one main point
- if needed, split into a short sequence

### Production pairing
- `baoyu-post-to-weibo`
- `summarize`
- `content-digest`

### Notes
- velocity matters more than completeness
- use as amplification, not as the only source of truth

---

## X / Twitter-style Thread

### Best for
- insight threads
- concise expert framing
- layered argument in short units

### Format guidance
- post 1 must carry the hook
- one thought per post
- sequence should escalate logically
- end with takeaway, question, or actionable synthesis

### Production pairing
- `baoyu-post-to-x`
- `summarize`
- `content-digest`

### Notes
- thread structure matters more than paragraph elegance

---

## PPT / Slides

### Best for
- teaching
- presentation
- one-page visual summaries
- content derivative assets

### Format guidance
- each slide should carry one strong message
- build visual hierarchy, not article paragraphs
- convert arguments into sections, comparisons, frameworks, and visual structure

### Production pairing
- `baoyu-slide-deck`
- `ppt-afp`
- `pptx-generator`
- `baoyu-image-gen`

### Notes
- choose between image-first slides and editable PPTX route deliberately

---

## Audio / Podcast Script

### Best for
- spoken delivery
- creator narration
- article-to-audio reuse

### Format guidance
- write for the ear, not the eye
- shorter sentences
- stronger transitions
- repeat anchors when needed

### Production pairing
- `podcast-script-generator`
- `podcast`
- `tts`
- `mm-voice-maker`

### Notes
- do not directly read a dense article aloud without rewriting
