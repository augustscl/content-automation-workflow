# Example: AI News Briefing Workflow

## Scenario
User wants a "今日 AI 新闻快讯" that covers the most important AI news from the last 24 hours.

## Five-layer mapping
- Input: search and fetch 1 to 3 high-value AI news stories
- Processing: identify the common thesis across the stories
- Production: create long-form briefing, short-post, cover image
- Distribution: prepare draft-ready article assets
- Deposition: save source list, thesis, outputs, and workflow observations

## Example thesis
AI competition is shifting from pure model performance toward product depth, compute control, and capital organization.

## Example outputs
- `ai-news-brief-YYYY-MM-DD.md`
- `ai-news-social-YYYY-MM-DD.md`
- `ai-news-deposition-YYYY-MM-DD.md`
